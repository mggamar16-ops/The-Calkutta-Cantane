A Note from the Kitchen

We know what changed.
Here's what you can expect.

The Calcutta Canteen has been part of this neighbourhood for a while now. We've served hospital staff on long shifts, students on tight budgets, and families on ordinary evenings — and we're genuinely grateful for every one of those guests.

We also know that we haven't always gotten everything right. A restaurant is built on trust, and trust is built slowly and broken quickly. We've spent the last several months not just redesigning the menu, but rethinking the way we operate — our service standards, our kitchen practices, and how we handle every guest interaction.

What's new: a dedicated Mediterranean menu, developed and tested over six months. A retrained front-of-house team. A reservation system. A commitment that when something goes wrong — we fix it.

We're not asking you to take our word for it. Come once, tell us what you think, and let the evening speak for itself.

The Calcutta Canteen Team, Durgapur
